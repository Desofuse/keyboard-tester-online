This website is a simple browser tool to check how your keyboard behaves. It highlights the keys you press and shows the raw keyboard event data ( event.code, event.key, keyCode, modifiers, and timestamps).

What it helps with

  • Confirm every key is detected (laptop keyboards, USB keyboards, gaming keyboards).
  • Find stuck or repeating keys.
  • Test shortcuts and modifier combos (Ctrl/Alt/Shift/Meta).
  • Check ghosting / key rollover behavior by holding multiple keys.
  • Understand layout differences (QWERTY vs AZERTY, ISO vs ANSI).

Privacy

The tester runs locally in your browser. It does not send your keystrokes to a server. See the Privacy Policy for details.
